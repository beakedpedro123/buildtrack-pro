# Privacy Policy for BuildTrack Pro

**Last Updated:** March 23, 2026

## Introduction

BuildTrack Pro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Employee Information:** Name, phone number, email address, hourly rate, PIN for clock-in/out
- **Job Information:** Project names, addresses, client names, contact information, budgets, dates
- **Time Tracking Data:** Clock-in/out times, job assignments, hours worked, labor costs
- **Safety Information:** Safety topics, meeting notes, attendance records, photos from safety meetings
- **Financial Data:** Labor costs, budget information, payroll details, expense records
- **Account Information:** Login credentials, user preferences, role assignments

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifier
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, actions performed, time spent in app, error logs
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates (if permitted) for job site tracking
- **Timestamps:** When data is created, modified, or accessed

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Enabling time tracking, job management, payroll processing, and reporting
- **Communication:** Sending updates, notifications, and support messages
- **Analytics:** Understanding how the app is used to improve features and performance
- **Safety & Compliance:** Maintaining safety records, compliance documentation, and audit trails
- **Legal Obligations:** Complying with labor laws, tax requirements, and other regulations
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Secure Servers:** Data is stored on secure, access-controlled servers
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access your data
- **Backups:** Regular backups are maintained for data recovery purposes
- **Retention:** Data is retained as long as your account is active, plus any period required by law

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers:** Third parties who help us operate the app (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize sharing with third parties

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Opt-Out:** Disable certain data collection features (e.g., location tracking)
- **Data Portability:** Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

## 6. Children's Privacy

BuildTrack Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

## 7. Third-Party Links & Services

The app may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.

## 8. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion, except where required by law
- **Legal Holds:** Data may be retained longer if required by legal proceedings or regulations

## 9. International Data Transfers

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other countries. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To submit a request, contact us using the information below.
