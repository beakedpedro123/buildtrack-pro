Getting Started with BuildTrack Pro

Everything you need to run your crew, track hours, manage budgets, and stay OSHA-compliant — all from one app.

1. Set Up Your Company

Download BuildTrack Pro, create your company account, and invite your crew. Each employee gets a unique PIN for clock-in and app access.

2. Add Your Jobs

Create jobs with addresses, budgets, and deadlines. Assign crew members to each job. Track progress in real-time from the dashboard.

3. GPS Time Tracking

Employees clock in/out with GPS verification. You see exactly who's on-site, when they arrived, and total hours — no more paper timesheets.

4. Budget & Labor Costs

Set budgets per job, track labor costs automatically as hours are logged. Get alerts before you go over budget. Export payroll reports as PDF.

5. Safety & Compliance

Run toolbox talks, log safety meetings with attendance, file daily field reports. Stay OSHA-ready with digital records your crew can sign.

6. Ask Pivot AI

Pivot is your built-in AI assistant. Ask it construction calculations, get meeting summaries turned into goals, or troubleshoot any app issue — in English or Spanish.

Need Help?

Switch to the Tickets tab to submit a support ticket — our team and Pivot AI will help resolve your issue. Or use the Pivot AI tab to chat directly with our AI assistant for instant answers.

Quick Tips

  • Use the Schedule tab to plan your crew's week and set daily goals
  • Run Meetings through the app — Pivot transcribes them and creates goals with deadlines
  • Export Payroll PDFs with overtime calculations for your accountant
  • The app works in English and Spanish — your crew can switch languages anytime
  • All data syncs in real-time — changes show up for your whole team within seconds
