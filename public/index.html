Everything Your Crew Needs

⏱️

GPS Time Tracking

Clock in/out with GPS verification. Foremen can clock in entire crews at once. Offline mode works even without signal — syncs when you're back online.

Real-Time Job Costing

See exactly where your money goes. Labor costs calculate automatically from clock entries. Track expenses, change orders, and budget burn-down in real time.

📋

Daily Field Reports

Foremen submit reports with photos, weather conditions, crew counts, and work completed. Management sees everything instantly — no more end-of-day phone calls.

Job profitability charts, labor cost trends, tax breakdowns (payroll, workers comp, liability), and budget burn-down graphs. Know your numbers before they become problems.

Meeting → Goals Pipeline

Record your meetings, get AI-generated summaries, and auto-create weekly goals with deadlines and assignees. Turn talk into action automatically.

5 roles with custom dashboards: Owner, Office Manager, Logistics, Foreman, and Laborer. Everyone sees exactly what they need — nothing more, nothing less.

Built-in safety topics library. Schedule and track toolbox talks, record attendance, and maintain compliance records — all from the app.

Works Everywhere

Native app for iOS and Android, plus a full web version. Your crew uses phones in the field, you use a computer in the office. Everyone stays connected.

PDF Reports & Sharing

Generate professional PDF reports for payroll, job budgets, and daily field reports. Share via email, text, or AirDrop. No QuickBooks required.
