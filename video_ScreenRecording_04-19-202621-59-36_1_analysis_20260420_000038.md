Based on the video, here is exactly what happens when the user tries to download a PDF from a message:

1.  **Attachment Appearance:** The attachment appears within the message as a dark rectangular box. Inside the box, there is a document icon on the left, the file name displayed in yellow text, and the instruction "Tap to download & open" below it. On the right side, there is a yellow circular button with a downward-pointing arrow, which serves as the download button.
2.  **User Action:** The user taps this yellow circular download button.
3.  **Resulting Behavior:** After tapping the button, a loading spinner briefly appears. Then, the screen flashes completely white for a moment before returning to the same "Messages" screen. The PDF does not download or open.

This exact sequence of events occurs twice in the video, with two different attachments ("SWANSON RES. SITE PLAN 4-7-2..." and "smiddy's plans august 12.pdf"). In both cases, the download attempt fails, resulting in a white screen flash and a return to the message list.
